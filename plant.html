The row having the plant names get breaks down first when I gradually compressing browser size. This happens is due to the certainty of the plant names are in larger divs with class col-md, which use more space than the smaller divs with in the class col-sm-2, which simply contains the letters. The larger divs are shifted to the next row as the screen size reduces since there was not enough space for them. On the other hand, the smaller letter-only divs can occupy a lesser area and remain on the same row for a long period of time.
